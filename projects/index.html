Mask Detection Monitor

A tool that uses Convolutional Neural Networks to calculate a Mask Score in a populated area.

I collaborated with Zeyad Mansour to create a Mask Detection Monitor for the 2021 HS Hack OSU Hacakthon. Our project uses OpenCV and Neural Networks to determine whether or not people are wearing masks.
